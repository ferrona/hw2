About Us

We’re here to integrate your home with innovative technologies.

Most people want to remain in their current residence and within their community for as long as possible.

For many, unfortunately, aging presents unprecedented challenges that prevent them from staying at home.

We believe that every individual has the right to choose how they live as they age, and technology offers a path to maximize independence, productivity, and social engagement.

Our Mission

To make aging in place possible for every aging adult through tech and human connection
